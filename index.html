Hey, I'm Emily.


I'm a PhD student in the Department of Operations, Information, and Decisions at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, starting Fall 2021. Previously, I studied Human-Computer Interaction at Stanford (BS '20, MS '21). My passion lies at the intersection of humans and technology: how technology makes us more productive, changes the way we communicate, and transforms organizations.

Writings on this website cover informal musings on a wide range of topics, as well as some personal updates.
