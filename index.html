Hey, I'm Emily.

I'm a PhD student in the Department of Operations, Information, and Decisions at the Wharton School, University of Pennsylvania, starting Fall 2021. I will be joining Professor Duncan Watts in the Computational Social Science Lab.

My research interest is in understanding the dynamics of human systems through computation — ranging from technology-mediated work to online decision-making groups — and applying these insights to the design of technology and organizations.

Previously, I earned my Bachelor's in Computer Science (2020) and my Master's in Symbolic Systems (2021) at Stanford University, specializing in human-computer interaction. I am grateful to have been advised by Professors Michael Bernstein and Melissa Valentine.
