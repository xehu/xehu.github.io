Hey, I'm Emily.


I study Human-Computer Interaction at Stanford (Bachelor's '20, Master's '21). My passion lies at the intersection of humans and technology. I am interested in ways that technology makes us more productive, changes the way we communicate, and transforms organizations. I am also interested in the legal, social, and political implications of technological advancement. I've done research in social computing and crowd-supported collaborative work.

I also love to write stuff! My writings cover technology, design, politics, travel, food, dance, and more.
