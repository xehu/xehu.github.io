Welcome to my blog!

For as long as I can remember, I've been a writer. On my first birthday, as with many Chinese children, my family presented me with an array of novel objects. Money. A map. A book. And so on. It's said that what I chose would define the person I would become.

I chose a pen.

To this day, I see that choice as no accident. I quickly began "writing" stories---dictating wild imaginations to my parents and grandparents, then, eventually, committing them to paper on my own. Writing is, for me, much more than a means to an end---much more than the assignments and the accolades, much more than a mere vessel for words.

Writing breathes life. And in this blog, whose code I'll also write (admittedly a very different kind of writing), I hope to convey a snapshot of my own life.
